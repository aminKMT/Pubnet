About Me

Interested in freight logistics and supply chain management, intelligent transportation systems, autonomous and connected vehicles, multimodal transportation systems, travel demand modeling, and transportation energy. Currently, working at Upper Great Plains Transportation Institute (UGPTI), where we work on various research topics, including but not limited to highway-rail grade crossing crash analysis and Routing and Scheduling Complex Railway Networks
